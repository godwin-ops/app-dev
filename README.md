# 📺 Stranger Things

> *"Friends don't lie."* – Eleven  

## Overview

**Stranger Things** is a sci-fi horror series created by the Duffer Brothers and produced by Netflix. It blends supernatural mystery, '80s nostalgia, and strong character development.

### 📅 Seasons

1. Season 1 – July 15, 2016  
2. Season 2 – October 27, 2017  
3. Season 3 – July 4, 2019  
4. Season 4 – May 27, 2022  
5. Season 5 – _Coming Soon_

---

## 🌟 Key Characters

- **Eleven** – Telekinetic girl with a mysterious past  
- **Mike Wheeler** – Loyal friend and natural leader  
- **Dustin Henderson** – Science geek with a big heart  
- **Lucas Sinclair** – Brave and skeptical  
- **Will Byers** – The boy who vanished  
- **Jim Hopper** – Tough but caring police chief  
- **Joyce Byers** – Determined and loving mother

---

### 🧪 Themes & Elements

- 👾 Parallel dimensions  
- 🔬 Government experiments  
- ❤️ Friendship & loyalty  
- 🌌 Retro pop culture
